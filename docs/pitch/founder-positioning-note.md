## 1. Executive Sponsorship Profile

The strategic vision, partnership development, and corporate governance of the **Iraq Digital Gateway (IDG)** platform are directed by the Founder:

### **Mustafa Jalal Khoshnaw**  
*Founder, Strategic Owner, and Executive Sponsor*

As the strategic owner of the IDG platform, Mustafa Jalal Khoshnaw is responsible for:
- Leading high-level vision, partnership strategies, and platform development policy.
- Aligning system parameters with regional authorities (KRG and Federal Iraq Ministries).
- Managing institutional engagement with strategic partners (such as the Rwanga Foundation).
- Championing bilateral data sovereignty treaties that protect regional records.

## 2. Distinction of Roles & Operational Execution
To maintain professional organizational standards, the platform strictly separates leadership and operations:

```
                  ┌──────────────────────────────────────────────┐
                  │          Mustafa Jalal Khoshnaw              │
                  │ Founder, Strategic Owner, Executive Sponsor   │
                  │  (Vision, Trust, Partnership, Governance)    │
                  └──────────────────────┬───────────────────────┘
                                         │
                                         ▼
                  ┌──────────────────────────────────────────────┐
                  │          Engineering & Operations            │
                  │   (Execution of Audits, Code Assemblies)     │
                  └──────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Operational Separation of Concerns:
- **Founder Role:** Directs overall system architecture and leads partnership discussions with KRG and Federal representatives.
- **Implementation Team:** Executes the concrete technical developments, code adjustments, server operations, database schema configurations, and automated QA audits.
- **Advantage:** By separating governance from database administration, we ensure that security audits and strategic agreements remain objective, compliant, and focused on national safety.
