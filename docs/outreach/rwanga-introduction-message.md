# Strategic Introduction Brief & Convening Inquiry — Rwanga Foundation

**Document Reference:** IDG-OUT-RWA-002  
**Status:** DRAFT PROPOSAL FOR DIALOGUE  

---

## 1. Context & Purpose
We are approaching the **Rwanga Foundation** because of your reputation for steering capacity building, promoting youth professional growth, and facilitating digital transformation initiatives within the Kurdistan Region. 

Our executive steering committee wishes to present the **Iraq Digital Gateway (IDG)** platform and explore possible cooperation in the form of convening and advisory introduction support.

---

## 2. Independence & Strict Operational Boundary
To maintain absolute security standards and avoid institutional confusion, we declare:
- **Strict Separation of Concerns:** This trading gate technology and customs reconciliation system is functionally isolated. It has no linkage, interface, or shared data with social programs, NGO registers, or civil relief resources.
- **No Assumed Endorsement:** We do not suggest or imply that the Rwanga Foundation has already approved, endorsed, or partnered with this project. This brief is purely a formal invitation designed to establish constructive, compliant dialogue.

---

## 3. Potential Scope of Cooperation
A strategic introduction partner can help bridge the gap between technical modernizers and executive representatives. We propose exploring the following activities:
- **Stakeholder Roundtable Convening:** Rwanga could host technical workshops, convening local trade groups and government technical specialists to review customs digital alignment protocols.
- **Youth Advisory Alignment:** Help design educational training paths to certify young Kurdish operators on handling modern, secure gateway platforms.
- **Administrative Transparency Facilitation:** Serving as a trusted, non-governmental advisor to review our strict data protection statements and general compliance reports.
