## 1. Demo Structure
This guide outlines the 20-minute live demonstration sequence designed for executive sponsors presenting the **Iraq Digital Gateway (IDG)** prototype.

---

## 2. Minute-by-Minute Sequence

### [0:00 - 0:02] 1. Platform Purpose & Strategic Objectives
- **Activity:** Open the presentation homepage with English/Arabic/Kurdish localized titles.
- **Narrative:** *"Distinguished guests, today we present the Iraq Digital Gateway. Our objective is simple: reduce cross-border trade friction, eliminate manual clearance delays, and prevent customs leaks by introducing a secure transaction validation system."*

### [0:02 - 0:04] 2. Sovereign Architecture & Isolation Boundaries
- **Activity:** Point out the separated Federal/KRG databases highlighted in the platform architectural brief.
- **Narrative:** *"We do not centralize data. Regional registers are physically and logically decoupled from Federal ones. Trust is established by architecture, not promises."*

### [0:04 - 0:06] 3. Admin vs. User Experience Separation
- **Activity:** Show the Admin portal controls, contrasting them with standard user workspaces.
- **Narrative:** *"System configuration and connection tracking are restricted to Technical Admins. Border operators work within clean, simplified workspaces localized to their language."*

### [0:06 - 0:08] 4. Executive Dashboards Demo
- **Activity:** Showcase the high-level metrics, showing how trade speed and volume trend charts are rendered.
- **Narrative:** *"Decision-makers can monitor macro KPIs. The figures represent regional aggregates without exposing individual corporate or personal entities."*

### [0:08 - 0:10] 5. Operational Dashboards Demo
- **Activity:** Transition to the local border checkpoint interface, showcasing manifest filing routines.
- **Narrative:** *"Border staff register clearance entries directly inside their localized panel. Validation runs instant role checks behind the scenes."*

### [0:10 - 0:12] 6. KRG Compatibility Presentation
- **Activity:** Show how KRG entries parse BRS schema definitions and verify KRDPASS login formats.
- **Narrative:** *"IDG is engineered from the ground up to respect local KRG laws, mapping directly to BRS registers and OIDC structures."*

### [0:12 - 0:14] 7. Joint Metadata-Only Model
- **Activity:** Show how the Joint Operations console displays cryptographic SHA-256 fingerprint hashes in place of raw demographic details.
- **Narrative:** *"The joint console verifies that a transaction was cleared with a safe cryptographic hash. Raw records never cross regional networks."*

### [0:14 - 0:16] 8. UAT Dry-Run Scenario
- **Activity:** Run a simulated clearance inside the simulator panel.
- **Narrative:** *"We run rigorous compliance scenarios to ensure routing enclaves handle approvals and rejections under standard data safeguards."*

### [0:16 - 0:18] 9. Provider Onboarding & Staging Requirements
- **Activity:** Point to the current status code: `CONDITIONALLY_READY — OUTREACH PACKAGE READY, PROVIDERS REQUIRED`.
- **Narrative:** *"The software is complete and linter-verified. As a safety constraint, active government servers are kept offline. Real-world connections will launch once physical API keys are issued."*

### [0:18 - 0:20] 10. Pilot Activation next steps
- **Activity:** Display the 45-minute technical agenda and DIT onboarding request packets.
- **Narrative:** *"The immediate next step is technical alignment with the Department of IT to establish our sandboxed staging network for sandbox testing. We invite your team to join."*
